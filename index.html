Understanding Antioxidants and Free Radicals

Antioxidants are molecules that inhibit the oxidation of other molecules, thereby preventing cell damage. They neutralize free radicalsâ€”unstable atoms that can damage cells, leading to aging and diseases. Free radicals are generated through normal metabolic processes and external sources like pollution and radiation. A diet rich in antioxidants can help combat oxidative stress and promote overall health.
